## Decision Flow

```
Step 1: VERIFY
    ↓
  Score?
    ├─ <70: STOP (kill idea)
    ├─ 70-84: GO (continue)
    └─ 85+: STRONG GO (high confidence)
    
Step 5: DECIDE
    ↓
  Metrics?
    ├─ RED: KILL (document lessons)
    ├─ YELLOW: PIVOT (reframe + retest)
    └─ GREEN: BUILD (proceed to Step 6)
    
Step 8: UNLOCK
    ↓
  Revenue?
    ├─ $0: Pivot monetization
    ├─ $1-5K: Continue
    └─ $5K+: Accelerate to Step 9
```
