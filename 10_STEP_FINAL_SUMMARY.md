- **Output:** GO/NO-GO decision

**Step 2: ANALYZE Market** (Days 4-10)
- Research 10 competitors (5 direct, 5 indirect)

- **Output:** Market intelligence brief

- Build landing page (no coding required)
- Implement 3-tier CTA strategy
- Create demo (video/screenshots)
- **Output:** Live landing page with analytics

- Drive 500+ visitors to landing page
- Conduct 15-20 user interviews
- Use "Mom Test" questions
- **Output:** 15-20 transcripts + conversion data

**Step 5: DECIDE Go/No-Go** (Days 31-35)
- Check 5 quantitative metrics (ALL required)
- Assess 4 qualitative signals (need 3+)
- Use decision calculator
- **Output:** GREEN (build) / YELLOW (pivot) / RED (kill)
