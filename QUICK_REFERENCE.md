## üìã The 10 Steps

### VALIDATE Phase (Weeks 1-5)

**1. VERIFY Problem** (Days 1-3)
- Problem statement
- Pain rating (need 7+/10)
- Feasibility (need 70+/100)
- GO/NO-GO decision

**2. ANALYZE Market** (Days 4-10)
- 10 competitors researched
- ICP defined
- Unit economics calculated
- Market intelligence brief

**3. LAUNCH Smoke Test** (Days 11-15)
- Landing page live
- 3-tier CTA strategy
- Wizard of Oz demo
- Analytics tracking

**4. INTERVIEW Users** (Days 16-30)
- 500+ visitors driven
- 15-20 interviews conducted
- Patterns documented
- Mom Test questions

**5. DECIDE Go/No-Go** (Days 31-35)
- 5 quantitative metrics checked
- 4 qualitative signals assessed
- GREEN/YELLOW/RED decision
- Evidence documented

---

### BUILD Phase (Weeks 6-16)

**6. ARCHITECT MVP** (Days 36-40)
- 3 must-have features defined
- Tech stack selected
- 10-20 beta users committed
- Scope explicitly cut

**7. BUILD with AI** (Weeks 6-10)
- Loveable.ai code generation
- Cursor AI iteration
- Vercel deployment
- Ship at 70%

**8. UNLOCK Feedback** (Weeks 11-16)
- Daily metrics monitored
- Weekly user interviews
- Rapid iteration
- First revenue activated

---

### SCALE Phase (Month 4+)

**9. INTEGRATE Partnerships** (Ongoing)
- Partner scorecard (50 points)
- Equity framework (5 factors)
- 3-month trial period
- Strategic integrations

**10. LEVERAGE Distribution** (Ongoing)
- Tier 1: Direct (0-10 users)
- Tier 2: Community (10-100)
- Tier 3: Scalable (100-1000+)
- Multiple channels

## ‚úÖ Success Criteria

**After Step 5 (Week 5):**
- 500+ visits
- 3-5% conversion
- 15+ interviews
- 30%+ would pay
- GREEN decision

**After Step 8 (Week 16):**
- 40%+ activation
- 30%+ retention
- $1-5K MRR

- $60-100K MRR
- 600-1000 users
- CAC < LTV/3
